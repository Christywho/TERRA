Crop Knowledge Base

Wheat

A staple food crop grown worldwide. Requires cool weather for growth.

  • Ideal pH: 6.0 - 7.0
  • Water: Moderate
  • Harvest: 120 days

Rice

Primary food source for half the world's population. Needs flooded fields.

  • Ideal pH: 5.5 - 7.0
  • Water: High
  • Harvest: 150 days

Corn (Maize)

Versatile crop used for food, feed, and fuel. High nutrient requirement.

  • Ideal pH: 5.8 - 7.0
  • Water: Moderate
  • Harvest: 90-120 days

Soybean

Legume mainly grown for oil and protein. Fixes nitrogen in soil.

  • Ideal pH: 6.0 - 6.8
  • Water: Moderate
  • Harvest: 100 days
